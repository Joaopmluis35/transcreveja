Help & Support

Welcome to the Ouviescrevi help page. Here are the most frequently asked questions:

1. What is Ouviescrevi?

It's an automated tool that converts audio and video files into text using artificial intelligence (AI).

2. Which file formats are supported?

We currently support .mp3, .mp4, .wav, .m4a, and other common formats.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No. Ouviescrevi is free to use and does not require registration.

4. Is the transcription 100% accurate?

Accuracy depends on audio quality and clarity of speech. We use OpenAI's Whisper model to ensure high-quality results.

5. Are my files stored?

No. All files are temporary and automatically deleted after transcription.
