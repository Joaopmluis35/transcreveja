🧠 What is Ouviescrevi?

Ouviescrevi is an online tool that uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to automatically transcribe audio and video, generate smart summaries, translate texts, correct mistakes, and even convert files like Word to PDF, image to text, and much more.

Ideal for teachers, journalists, students, companies and anyone who needs to turn voice into text quickly and for free.

You don't need to install anything. Just drag a file, record your voice or paste the text — and we'll do the rest. 🚀

Main features:

🎙️ Audio/video transcription (with Whisper AI)

🧠 Automatic text and PDF summarisation

🌍 Translation into multiple languages

📚 Automatic content classification

📁 Converter for Word, PDF, Images, etc.
